Los Angeles Unified School District (LAUSD)

Arts Equity Index for Secondary Schools data (pulled 4/4/17)

The Arts Equity Index (AEI) categorizes all LAUSD schools (K-12) based on the scope of their provided arts instruction, arts resources, arts professional development, Title 1 Status, and Student Equity Index (Poverty, Foster Students, English Language Learners).

The dataset was last refreshed April 2017.

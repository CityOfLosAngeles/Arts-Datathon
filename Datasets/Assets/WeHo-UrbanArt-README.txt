City of West Hollywood WeHo Arts

City of West Hollywood Urban Art Locations data (pulled 4/10/17)

The City of West Hollywood’s Percent for Art Program includes works by Billy Al Bengston, Shepard Fairey, and Ball-Nogues. Over 60 artworks are included in this dataset with links to photos for each artwork.

The dataset was last refreshed in October 2015. It was originally published on the City of West Hollywood Open Data Portal.
